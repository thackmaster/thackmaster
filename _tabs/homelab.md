# My Setup
My local setup consists of the following:
- Synology DS920+
    - 4 WD Red HDs (3 4TBs, 1 12TBs)
    - 8 GB RAM
- 2 HP EliteDesk 800 G2 (mini)
    - Intel Core i5 vPro
    - 8 GB RAM
    - 256 GB SSD
    - Debian 11

- [Bookstack](https://www.bookstackapp.com/) (Wiki platform organized like "books")
